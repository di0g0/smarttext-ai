Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 9, 2025

Introduction

SmartText AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our macOS application.

Information We Collect

SmartText AI is designed with privacy in mind:

  • Text Processing: Text you translate or improve is sent to OpenAI's API for processing. We do not store or log this text on our servers.
  • Local Storage: Translation history is stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this data.
  • Usage Data: We collect minimal, anonymized usage data (daily translation count) stored locally to enforce free tier limits.
  • Purchase Information: In-app purchases are processed by Apple. We receive only transaction verification from Apple's servers.

How We Use Information

  • To provide translation and text improvement services via OpenAI's API
  • To manage your subscription and enforce usage limits
  • To improve the app's functionality and user experience

Third-Party Services

SmartText AI uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI API: For text translation and AI-powered improvements. Subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store: For payment processing. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Security

All communications with OpenAI's API are encrypted using industry-standard HTTPS. Your translation history is stored locally on your device and is protected by your device's security measures.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your locally stored translation history
  • Delete your translation history at any time through the app
  • Request information about your data
  • Delete the app and all associated data

Children's Privacy

SmartText AI does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
